Frequently Asked Questions

Earnings can be withdrawn navigating to the Sales dashboard and selecting 'Initiate Transfer'. Funds are typically processed within 2-3 business days to your connected bank account.

To enable international shipping, go to Settings > Logistics. You will need to define your shipping zones and configure carrier rates for each region to ensure accurate checkout pricing.

Reviews can be monitored from your Dashboard. You can respond publicly to feedback to maintain your brand reputation. For inappropriate reviews, use the 'Report' flag for our moderation team to review.
